Armenian Tortilla Pizza
Take This Dish and Twist It
Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
1 lb. ground turkey
1 tsp. salt
2 cups tomato chunks, withoutjuice
1 tsp. cumin
1 large onion, chunked
2 Tbls. parsley
6 oz. can tomato paste
3 cloves garlic
1 sweet pepper, chunked
fresh ground pepper
8 medium (or 4 large) flour tortillas
Pulse garlic, onion, tomato, and sweet pepper in food
processor until chopped ﬁne. Pour off any excess
juice. Place in large bowl and mix with all other
ingredients. Spread onto tortillas to the edges. Bake
on rack in 400° oven for 10 minutes. (You should be
careful to place cookie sheet under these as they may
drip.) Sen/e with shredded lettuce, salsa, sour cream,
or chopped tomatoes.
