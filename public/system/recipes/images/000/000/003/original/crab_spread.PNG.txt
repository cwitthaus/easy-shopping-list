Crab Spread
Cindy Eckelkamp
Prep Time: 10 minutes
8 01. cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp. garlic salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
6 01. crab meat, chapped
1/2 cup mild Cheddar cheese, shredded
Mix all ingredients. Chill. Serve with crackers or
vegetables.
NOTE: Add milk if too thick.
