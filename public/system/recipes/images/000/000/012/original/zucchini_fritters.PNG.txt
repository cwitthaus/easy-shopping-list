Zucchini Fritters
American Profile 6-2007
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes

1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1 cup cornmeal
2 tsp. baking powder
1/3 cup flour
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 cup grated Parmesan
1/4 cup sour cream
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1 Tbls. olive oil
1/4 tsp. red pepper (optional)
