Peanut Butter & Jelly Bread Pudding
Cyrus
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time 50 minutes
4 cups milk
1/2 cup sugar
6 eggs
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup peanut butter
1 Tbls. vanilla
1 cup favorite jelly
8 cups bread cubed
Mix with peanut butter, sugar, eggs, vanilla and salt in
blender. Add half of milk and blend again. In large
bowl combine egg mixture, remaining milk, and bread,
tossing gently. Spoon mixture into 8"X12" greased
baking dish. Spoon teaspoons of jelly over dessert.
Let stand 10 minutes or up to 4 hours in refrigerator.
Bake at 350Â° for 1 hour until knife inserted in center
comes out clean Serve warm or chilled
