Grilled Lime Chicken
The Global Gourmet
Prep Time: (15 min.)
3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
3 medium cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup brown sugar
3 Tbls. Dijon mustard
1/4 cup cider vinegar
2 or 3 Tbls. lime juice
1 or 2 Tbls. lemon juice
6 Tbls. olive oil
Mix all ingredients except chicken. Add chicken. Allow
to marinate for at least 30 minutes. Brush marinate on
chicken while grilling.
