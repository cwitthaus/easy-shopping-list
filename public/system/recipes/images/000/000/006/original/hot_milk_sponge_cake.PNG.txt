Hot Milk Sponge Cake
Helen Eckelkamp
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 45 minutes
4 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
2 cups sugar
1 cup milk
1 3/4 cups plus 2 Tbls. flour
2 Tbls. butter
2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
Beat eggs. Gradually add sugar. Beat until light and
ï¬‚uffy. Add dry ingredients and vanilla. Heat milk and
butter in microwave until almost boiling (about 2
minutes); Stir quickly into batter. Pour immediately into
9-inch or 10-inch tube pan. Bake at 350 degrees for
40 to 45 minutes. Invert pan to cool. Remove from
pan when cool.
